1. SELLER PSYCHOLOGY SUMMARY
Mental State Profile
Daily Reality:

Opens dashboard 5-8 times per day during business hours
Each session lasts 30-90 seconds (scan ‚Üí decide ‚Üí act)
High stress during peak hours (10 AM - 2 PM, 4 PM - 6 PM)

Decision Speed: Must see critical info in <3 seconds
Cognitive Capacity: Processing 3-5 KPIs max before overload
Visual Memory: Relies on position consistency ("I know wallet is always top-right")
Seller Goals (Priority Order):

Survive: Don't run out of cash (wallet balance, COD settlements)
Prevent Loss: Minimize RTO, catch failed shipments early
Grow Revenue: Increase orders, expand geographically
Optimize Costs: Find cheaper couriers, reduce RTO rate
Seller Fears (Loss Aversion Psychology):

Wallet empty = business stops (can't create new shipments)

Incorrect courier choice = higher costs + delivery delays
